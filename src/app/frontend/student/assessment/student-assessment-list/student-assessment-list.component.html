Insturctions
  1. The invigilator is authorized to verbally warn/move the student or seize the answer script if cheating occurs.
  2. Unruly behavior of the students toward the instructor/invigilator is considered as a serious offence and doing so, he will be sent out of examination hall and also will not be allowed to sit for further attempts.
  3. Students shall not leave the examination room temporarily during the period of the examination.
  4. Students are required to bring their own items and when examination is in progress students are not allowed to borrow any of items from other students.
  5. The use of mobile phones is not permitted in the examination hall and students must switch off their mobile phones before entering the examination hall and they have to submit the same with the invigilator. If the student is taking exam on his/her mobile phone, he/she should carry only one phone with themand second phone is not allowed in the hall.
  6. Students are not allowed to keep books, papers or other aids with them during the examination .Bags and other such articles must be left in designated areas near the examination hall.
  7. Students who wish to attract the attention of the instructor/invigilator during the examination shall do so by raising their hand and he/she will not be allowed to get up from the allotted seat.
  8. Students are reminded that cheating in an examination by any means is considered a serious offence which would lead to expulsion from the examination. The following would be treated ascheatingduring an examination:
    • Communicating or trying to communicate in any way with fellow students
    • Attempting to view examination papers of others
    • Using unapproved examination materials, such as: calculators, mobile phones, formulas
    • Possessing “cheat sheets”
    • Writing on desk, body parts, dress, scale or in any other materials
  9. If a student is caught cheating (irrespective of the extent) in any form his/her script will be marked “zero” in that examination, he/she will have to leave the examination room immediately and no makeup examination will be taken later.
  10. If any student is found cheating during the exam, his/her conduct will be punished by the instructor/invigilator. In case of any type of difficulty to do so it may be referred to the “Academic Misconduct Committee”.
  11. Read the Question and options very carefully before you chose to select a given option, once you select the option of choice you will not be allowed to edit or correct the same. After selecting an option provided it will show immediately whether you have marked right or wrong and if you feel 100% that the answer you have chosen is right and if you find the mistake regarding options given, you can submit your query by clicking the button “report mistake” so that it will be evaluated after the exam .
